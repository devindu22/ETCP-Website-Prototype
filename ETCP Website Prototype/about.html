Commitment to Sustainability

Our Mission

At ETCP, our mission is simple: to empower eco-conscious travellers and providers to create meaningful, sustainable experiences. We believe travel should not only inspire but also protect the planet and uplift the communities we visit. Through our innovative cloud-based platform, we’re making it easier than ever to explore the world responsibly.

What Sets Us Apart

ETCP is more than just a booking platform — it’s a community-driven ecosystem designed with sustainability at its core. Here’s what makes us unique:

  • Eco-Explorer Network (EEN): A space for eco-tourism providers to showcase their offerings, manage bookings, and highlight their sustainability efforts.
  • Eco-Discovery Hub: A dynamic search tool that lets you find experiences based on your preferences — from wildlife watching to volunteering — all with a focus on eco-friendly practices.
  • Eco-Journeys: Your personalized dashboard to manage bookings, discover similar experiences, and build your eco-travel wish-list.
  • ETCP Voyager: An interactive map and planner to craft your ideal eco-itinerary, complete with sustainability impact estimates.
  • Settings & Personalization: Customize your experience with nature-inspired themes, language options, and easy subscription management.

Our Team

ETCP was born out of a passion for both technology and the environment. Based in Sri Lanka, our team brings together local expertise in sustainability with cutting-edge cloud solutions. We’re a group of innovators, nature lovers, and community advocates dedicated to making eco-tourism accessible and impactful. From our developers to our sustainability experts, every member of the ETCP team is committed to creating a platform that serves travellers while supporting the planet.

Supporting Local Communities

We believe eco-tourism should benefit everyone — especially the local communities that make these experiences possible. That’s why ETCP promotes providers who prioritize sustainability and community engagement. By choosing ETCP, you’re not just booking a trip; you’re contributing to a global movement that values people, culture, and the environment.

Join Us

Whether you’re a traveler seeking your next eco-adventure or a provider looking to share your sustainable offerings, ETCP is here to help you make a difference. Together, we can create a future where travel is a force for good. Explore. Connect. Protect. Welcome to the ETCP community.
